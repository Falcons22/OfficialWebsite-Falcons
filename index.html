ABOUT US

Assailing Falcons is one of the most distinguished members of SAE International. The team brings together engineers from a variety of backgrounds that are involved in aircraft design and manufacturing, providing them with professional expertise and hands-on knowledge.

OUR MISSION

As a team, we aim to grab the highest laurels for the country and construct aerodynamically efficient aircrafts. We tend not only to achieve our mission statement of the competition but also learn and gather deep knowledge about avionics and aeronautics for further field development.
